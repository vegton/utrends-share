Question Creation Tips
Be Specific and Clear

Ask questions that have clear outcomes and can be answered with predictions.

Include a Timeframe

When possible, specify when the predicted event will occur or be resolved.

Choose the Right Category

Selecting the appropriate category helps people find questions they're interested in.

Avoid Biased Wording

Frame questions neutrally to get diverse predictions from the community.
